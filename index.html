Cybersecurity Awareness for a Safer Pakistan

An independent awareness project led by Muhammad Ehtisham — Cybersecurity Student & (ISC)2 Certified Professional

🌐 Our Vision: A Secure Digital Pakistan

Cybersecurity is no longer optional — it’s essential for every citizen, organization, and institution. Through this personal initiative, I aim to promote a culture of digital safety and cyber hygiene across Pakistan.

My mission is to simplify cybersecurity for everyday users — from students and teachers to small business owners and professionals — by delivering clear, actionable guidance that empowers people to protect themselves in the digital world.

💡 What You’ll Learn in Our Sessions

  • 🔐 The danger of weak/reused passwords – and how to create strong, secure ones
  • 🎣 How to spot phishing messages, fake websites, and social engineering scams
  • 📡 Staying protected on public Wi-Fi networks (cafés, airports, schools)
  • 🧼 Practical cyber hygiene tips: keeping devices updated, using 2FA, and more

These sessions are interactive and real-life based — designed to inform, not scare. You’ll leave with a stronger understanding of how to stay safe online, regardless of your technical background.

👨‍💻 Who Is Behind This Initiative?

I’m Muhammad Ehtisham, a final-year BS Cybersecurity student at Air University, and a freelance cybersecurity professional. I hold the globally recognized (ISC)2 Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) credential.

I believe every Pakistani deserves to understand how to protect themselves online. Whether it’s personal privacy, digital finance, or business data — I help people build a smarter, safer relationship with technology.
